Who Are We

Clean Sky Foundation is a forward-thinking organization founded with the mission to revolutionize environmental cleanup through the integration of drone technology and community engagement. Established to address the growing challenge of litter and pollution, Clean Sky Foundation combines drone-assisted trash detection with a volunteer-driven platform to make cleanup efforts efficient, accessible, and rewarding. Our mission is to foster cleaner communities, promote sustainable behaviors, and inspire collective action toward a healthier planet. Since its inception, Clean Sky Foundation has successfully raised awareness about waste management through gamified incentives, community events, and data-driven insights. By leveraging innovative solutions and collaborative efforts, we continue to make a tangible impact on the environment while empowering individuals to contribute to meaningful change.

Our Services

Clean Sky Foundation is committed to creating a cleaner, healthier planet by leveraging advanced drone technology and fostering community-driven action. Our mission is to streamline environmental cleanup efforts, inspire collective responsibility, and promote sustainable habits through innovative and engaging solutions. We envision a world where technology and community collaboration work together to eliminate litter and pollution, preserving natural and public spaces for future generations.
